Jaar 1

Planting

The cuttings are prepared and potted to start growing in a greenhouse. After six to nine months they are old and strong enough to be planted in the field.

Your trees produces it’s first pods in three to four years. This means that you’ll receive harvesting profits and returns from that year onwards.

From the seventh year your tree is considered adult (unlike your child). From here on out the tree will provide a steady return each year.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Each investment comes with risks, as does this one. The risks of investing in Pongamia trees are described in detail on our help page. If you still have questions, feel free to contact us.

Once a year the yield profit of the trees is paid out. When the payment takes place, you will receive a message from us with an overview and a choice receive the profits or reinvest them into new trees.

What to do with your profits is up to you. We provide two options:

  1. - Reveive your profits directly on your bank account.
  2. - Reinvest your profits into new trees. This makes you exponentially greener.

The post NURSERY OF THE TREE appeared first on COREKEES.

]]> All our trees start as cuttings, cut from Pongamia mother trees. These cuttings are planted by hand in special pots and placed in the ‘nursery’

After 4 to 6 weeks in the greenhouse, the cuttings are in strength and are planted in larger pots. From then on, they are allowed to continue to grow quietly outside until they are big enough to be planted on the land.

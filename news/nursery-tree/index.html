NURSERY OF THE PLANT

Steve Miranda24 June 2021News

The optimized climate in the greenhouse ensures that the cuttings catch on well and start to grow quickly. 🌱

In the greenhouse it is about 50 degrees. To give the trees the best possible start, they are sprayed every minute for 10 seconds by fog rain. This is how they grow best in the warm Paraguayan climate.
